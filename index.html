My design trifecta for Circular, Ethical, and Community-driven solutions:

Empathize

• Dig into behaviors, structures, and lived experiences across touchpoints
• Uncover the gaps, frictions, and opportunities that shape real-world interactions

Simplify

Innovate

• Design services, systems, and experiences that are equitable, adaptable, and regenerative • Build with people, not just for them, with an awareness of long-term impact • Create meaningful change that works in the real world
